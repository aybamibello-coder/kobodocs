How this is calculated

Nigeria Customs Service assesses duty using the CIF method: Cost (FOB) + Insurance + Freight, converted to Naira at the prevailing CBN exchange rate. Import duty is your HS code's rate applied to CIF. A 7% surcharge applies to the duty amount, ETLS (ECOWAS Trade Liberalisation Scheme) is 0.5% of CIF, and a 4% FCS/Import Levy applies to FOB. VAT (7.5%) is then applied to the sum of CIF, duty, surcharge, ETLS, and the levy.

This is a general estimate, not a customs assessment. Actual duty depends on your exact HS code classification, the CBN rate on your clearance date, and Customs' own valuation — confirm with a licensed clearing agent before shipping.
