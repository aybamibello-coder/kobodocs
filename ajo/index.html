    How it works

    No disputes over who paid, or who's next

    Does KoboDocs hold or move the money?

    No. This tool only keeps the record — rotation order and who's paid. Contributions still happen directly between members, the way your circle already does it.

    Is this saved anywhere?

    Saved automatically on this device — closing the tab or refreshing won't lose it. It's not yet shareable across devices, so other members can't see live updates unless you send them a PDF or WhatsApp update. Persistent, shareable circles are coming to Pro.

    Who collects next?

    The first member in your list who hasn't collected yet, in the order you set them — reorder anyone with the arrow buttons.
