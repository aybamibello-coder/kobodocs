What this is — and isn't

This generates a properly formatted draft affidavit in the standard Nigerian format. You'll still need to swear it before a Commissioner for Oaths at a High Court registry or notary — this tool prepares the document, it doesn't replace that step.

This is a drafting aid, not legal advice. For anything with significant consequences, have a lawyer review the final document.
