Land Sale Agreement / Deed of Assignment Generator

A properly structured Deed of Assignment for a land transaction — a document to take to a lawyer, not a substitute for one.

Read this before you use this tool Land fraud is a serious, common problem in Nigeria. This tool generates a draft document in the standard format — it does not verify the seller's title, confirm the land isn't sold to multiple buyers, check for government acquisition, or replace a licensed surveyor's report. Before any money changes hands: verify title at the State Lands Registry, obtain a search report, use a licensed surveyor, and have a qualified property lawyer review everything and handle Governor's Consent where required. Before you sign anything

  • Verify the seller's title at the State Lands Registry — confirm they actually own it and it isn't already sold, gifted, or under litigation.
  • Get a professional search report, not just a verbal assurance.
  • Use a licensed surveyor to confirm the exact plot and boundaries match the survey plan.
  • Check whether the land is affected by government acquisition or a road/utility setback.
  • Have a qualified property lawyer review this document and handle Governor's Consent where the land requires it.
  • Never pay the full purchase price before these checks are complete.
