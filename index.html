Questions

Before you start

The things people usually ask before creating their first document.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Every free tool works without signing up — open it, fill it in, download the PDF. An account is only needed for Pro features like saved clients and document history.

Is my data stored anywhere?

On the free tier, everything is processed in your browser for that session only — nothing is saved once you close the tab. Pro accounts securely store saved clients, employees, and document history.

Does KoboDocs hold or move my Ajo/Esusu money?

No. KoboDocs only keeps the record — rotation order, who's paid, who's next. Contributions are still made directly between members; we never touch or hold funds.

Are the payslip tax figures accurate?

The PAYE, pension and NHF presets reflect current published rates, but tax rules change and individual circumstances vary — always confirm final figures with your accountant or FIRS before relying on them for compliance.

What does Pro cost, and can I cancel anytime?

Pro is ₦1,500/month or ₦12,000/year. Business is ₦5,000/month or ₦45,000/year. Both are month-to-month or annual with no lock-in — cancel whenever you like.

Is KoboDocs a bank or a licensed lender?

No. KoboDocs is a document and record-keeping tool. It doesn't disburse loans, hold deposits, or move money on anyone's behalf.
