Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy is a general template describing how KoboDocs currently handles data. It is not legal advice, and hasn't been reviewed by a Nigerian lawyer — it should be before you rely on it for NDPA/NDPR compliance or any formal representation to users.

1. What we collect

KoboDocs is designed so most tools work without an account. Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Account data: email address and password (hashed), if you create a KoboDocs account for Pro, Business Suite, or Cooperative Plan features.
  • Business data you enter: business name, client details, employee/payslip information, invoice and document contents — stored in our database only if you're signed in and using a paid or account-linked feature. Free-tier tools store this only in your browser (localStorage), never on our servers.
  • Payment data: we never see or store your card details. Payments are processed by Paystack (and, where applicable, Flutterwave or Moniepoint); we only receive confirmation that a payment succeeded, plus a transaction reference.
  • Usage data: basic technical information (browser type, general location from IP, pages visited) for reliability and abuse prevention.

2. How we use it

  • To provide the document generation, Ajo/Esusu tracking, relocation calculators, and Business Suite features you use.
  • To process payments and activate the correct plan on your account.
  • To send service-related communication (e.g. payment reminders you configure, trial expiry notices).
  • To maintain and improve the reliability of the platform.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your business or client data to train any models.

3. Who we share it with

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider, which stores account and business data.
  • Cloudflare — hosts the website and processes traffic for performance and security.
  • Paystack (and Flutterwave/Moniepoint where used) — processes payments; they hold your payment details, not us.

We don't share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Ajo/Esusu and financial data

KoboDocs only keeps records — rotation order, contribution logs, dispute notes. We never hold, move, or have access to the actual funds contributed between circle members.

5. Your rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You can delete most of your own data directly from your account settings, or contact us at hello@kobodocs.com.ng for anything else.

6. Data retention

We keep account and business data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, associated data is removed from our active database, though some records may be retained briefly for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

7. Children's privacy

KoboDocs is intended for business owners, freelancers, and adults planning relocation. It is not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: hello@kobodocs.com.ng.
