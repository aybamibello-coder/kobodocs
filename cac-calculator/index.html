Why this is a range, not one number

CAC fees have changed more than once recently, and figures reported by different sources still don't fully agree even in 2026. This calculator uses commonly reported ranges as a planning estimate — not a live fee lookup. Limited company registration also adds stamp duty calculated on your share capital, and larger share capital increases both the CAC fee and stamp duty owed.

Always confirm the exact current fee on CAC's official portal (cac.gov.ng) before paying — this tool is for budgeting, not a substitute for the official fee schedule.
