How this is calculated

PAYE follows the Nigeria Tax Act's 2026 progressive bands: 0% on the first ₦800,000 of annual taxable income, then 15%, 18%, 21%, 23%, and 25% on higher bands. Pension is 8% of basic + housing + transport. NHF is 2.5% of basic salary. Rent relief (20% of annual rent, capped at ₦500,000) reduces taxable income if you enter your annual rent paid.

This is a general estimate, not tax advice — your employer's actual payroll calculation is the authoritative figure.
